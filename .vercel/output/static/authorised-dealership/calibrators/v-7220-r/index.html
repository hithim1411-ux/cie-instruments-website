About This Instrument

The Vartech V 7220 R is a professional source-and-measure temperature and process calibrator. It can both source (output) and measure DCV, Resistance (Ohms), Thermocouple signals (multiple T/C types) and RTD signals (various RTD types — 2-wire, 3-wire and 4-wire configurations). A clear 5-digit display with backlight makes field use easy. Additional features include Auto Power Off, On/Off detection and a built-in torch for working in low-light environments. The V 7220 R is designed for calibrating temperature transmitters, RTD sensors and thermocouple-based instruments in industrial and laboratory settings. Full specifications are available in the product catalogue.

Key Features

  • Source & Measure: DCV, Resistance (Ohms), Thermocouple (various types), RTD (2W / 3W / 4W)
  • 5-digit display with backlight
  • Thermocouple support — multiple T/C types
  • RTD support — 2-wire, 3-wire and 4-wire configurations
  • On/Off detection
  • Torch function for low-light environments
  • Auto Power Off
